Cherry Pie Recipe

Description

O Hi yo! This here is my favorite piece of pie. And despite the looks of it, the making of this pie... was no piece of cake. A very scrumptious baked pie, gushing with cherry paste that'll make you drool at the mere sight of it. Anyways enough of my rambling. Here's the recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup of sugar
  • 4 cups of sweet/sour cherries
  • 2 spoons of lemon juice
  • 2 tbsps of butter
  • 1/2 tsp of ground cinnamon
  • Homemade pie crust
  • 1 large egg white, beat with fork
  • granulated sugar for sprinkling

Steps

  1. Pit and halve cherries, and mix them with sugar and lemon juice to toss.
  2. Cook cherries in medium heat, until it produces a sauce
  3. Use spoon to scoop cherries into bowl, and mix with cornstarch until dissolved
  4. Return cherries into pot and cook until the sauce thickens
  5. Set aside cherries to cool, and prepare pie crust in the meantime
